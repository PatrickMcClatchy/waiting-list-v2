Settings Guide

📅 Scheduled Opening Times

Opens the list automatically and clears entries (backup is created).

Format: Day HH:MM (24h)

Examples:

  • Monday 09:00
  • Monday 09:00,Thursday 14:00
  • Monday 08:30,Wednesday 13:15,Friday 16:00

🚫 Closed List Message

Shown when sign-ups are closed. Suggest including:

  • Next opening time
  • Contact details

✅ Success Message

Shown after sign-up. Use {{position}} to show queue number.

Recommend including:

  • Confirmation
  • Next steps
  • Contact info

📄 Confirmation PDF

Downloadable after sign-up. Suggest including:

  • What the list means
  • Contact info
  • Expected waiting time

Note: Keep under 2MB. Replaces any existing PDF.
